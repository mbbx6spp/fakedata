Fake Data

How have you seen data misused or badly represented to make a biased case?

Perhaps you have just seen it misused due to misunderstanding?

If so, please contribute your story to this site via Github.

Contribute

To contribute a story to this website you can fork the mbbx6spp/fakedata repository on Github, add your story to the site's source on branch gh-pages and submit a pull request to the mbbx6spp:gh-pages fork/branch.

Thanks!
